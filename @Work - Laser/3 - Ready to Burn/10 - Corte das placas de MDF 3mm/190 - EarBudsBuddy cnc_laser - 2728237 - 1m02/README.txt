EarBudsBuddy cnc/laser by ZenziWerken on Thingiverse: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2728237

Summary:
It's a simple accessory to store classic earbuds. Like my "Wide Tooth Comb" I used thin birch plywood with macassar veneer. A clear spray coat makes it waterresistant.I used Solidedge to create the drawing and Fusion360 to extrude and roundover the edges. The stl is not tested yet. The build was done on a desktop cnc-machine by Stepcraft.Visit https://www.zenziwerken.de/en/ for more interesting designs.
