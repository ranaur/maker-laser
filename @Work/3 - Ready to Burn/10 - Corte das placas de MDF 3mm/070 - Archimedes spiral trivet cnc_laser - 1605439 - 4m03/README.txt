Archimedes spiral trivet cnc/laser by ZenziWerken on Thingiverse: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:1605439

Summary:
This trivet design is based on an Archimedes Spiral. It shows the advantages of computerized over manual production.My build was done on a desktop cnc-machine by Stepcraft from 6 mm birch plywood using a spiral-toothed 1.2 mm flute. I used dark wood stain on one part to create the strong contrast.Visit https://www.zenziwerken.de/ for more interesting designs.
